CS4349 audio CODEC

Required properties:

  - compatible : "cirrus,cs4349"

  - reg : the woke I2C address of the woke device for I2C

Optional properties:

  - reset-gpios : a GPIO spec for the woke reset pin.

Example:

codec: cs4349@48 {
        compatible = "cirrus,cs4349";
        reg = <0x48>;
        reset-gpios = <&gpio 54 0>;
};
